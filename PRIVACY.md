# Privacy Policy — Inkdex

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

**Inkdex collects nothing and sends nothing.**

- All data (your ChatGPT / Claude usage counts) is stored **locally** in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`, on your device only.
- Inkdex makes **no network requests**. It has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.
- No data is ever collected, transmitted, sold, or shared.

## Permissions

- **storage** — save your usage counts locally on your device.
- **clipboardWrite** — copy your generated Wrapped card to your clipboard.
- **host access (claude.ai, chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com)** — read message activity on those pages while you visit them, in order to count your usage. Read-only, on those sites only. Nothing is sent anywhere.
